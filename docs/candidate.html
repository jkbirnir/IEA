Pre-election candidate information

The below tables is represented by Figure … in the paper and includes national level information on candidates competing for the office of the President and Vice President, district level information for the lower chamber of the legislature (DPR), and provincial level for the upper level of the legislature (DPD). Information for each office at each level and election are provided in a separate sheet in a .csv format and can be accessed by clicking on the pertinent hyper-link. The information for a given level includes standardized identifyers including election year, name of office, provincial level identifiers and where relevant district level identifiers as articulated in version 1 (V1) of the Codebook.V1. For longitudinal analysis of multiple elections the information from discrete sheets can, therefore, be easily combined.
